I am Gerard H

Gerard H started his career in early 2008 as a DJ touring the London & Ibiza underground scenes, concentrating on the Funky, Electro, Progressive, Fidget & sometimes Tech House genres.

Since then, he took to the studio to produce and release music under the aliases of Gee-4rce, GerarDroid & now Gerard H on many different labels world wide, including his own project - Digital Storm Records.

After a few years' break, he's now returned in full force, taking on the Techno scene in both the record mixing and producing disciplines.
